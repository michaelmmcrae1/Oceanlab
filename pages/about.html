Why Oceanlab?

This website is a suppliment to foreign language classes inability to provide the most important necessity for learning language: exposure. Anybody who has ever studied language to at least above an intermediate level knows that without consistent exposure outside of class it is impossible to improve at a reasonable rate. Fluency not only requires tens of thousands of different words, but also thousands of hours of hearing the same words repeated in different contexts. This is of course something language classes cannot provide, even though they may claim to, and even though many students may take them under the impression that they can.

Oceanlab aims to provide at least a portion of those lost hours by using an SRS (spaced repitition system). An SRS is essentially a flashcard emulator. With Oceanlab, a teacher may create a deck of cards to be reviewed by their students. This assures that students are putting in the time needed outside of class to actually learn the language.
